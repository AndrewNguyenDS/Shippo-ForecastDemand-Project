"This project found the solution for problem in shipper capacity of Shippo. HR needs to know how many shipper we need for next week?
Thus, this project will follow some tasks:
1/ Forecast demand for Shippo by City and District
2/ Calculate the capacity of shippo if we don't change anything in number of shipper
3/ Find the number of shipper we need 
4/ Send the notification for HR
It brings many effect to HR and operation of Shippo. Great new for Shippo and Operation Research team"
